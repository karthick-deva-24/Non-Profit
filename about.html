About Goodwish

Our journey of impact and the vision that drives us forward.

Who We Are

Dedicated to Global Impact

Goodwish is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering sustainable development and providing essential aid to those in need. For over a decade, we have been at the forefront of social change.

Founded in 2012 by a group of passionate activists, we started with a single classroom and have grown into a global movement supported by thousands of donors. Our story is one of perseverance and hope.

Our Mission

To empower communities by providing educational and health opportunities.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has the chance to thrive and succeed.

Our History

Our Journey Since 2012

2012 - The Humble Beginning

Started with 3 volunteers and oru small classroom in a village. Our first mission was to provide books to 50 children.

2018 - Expanding Horizons

Reached 10 different countries. We introduced our healthcare programs and built our first mobile clinic.

2026 - Present Day Impact

Now a global community of 25k+ supporters. We serve thousands of families daily across 5 major service domains.

What Drives Us

🌟 Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and every life we touch.

Compassion

Compassion is the heartbeat of everything we do. We see every person we serve not as a statistic, but as a human being with dignity, dreams, and potential. Our volunteers and staff are trained to lead with empathy — listening first, acting with care, and treating every family with the respect they deserve.

  • We listen before we act — understanding needs at the community level
  • Every beneficiary is treated with dignity and full respect
  • Emotional support & counselling included in our relief efforts
  • Over 5,000 volunteers trained in compassion-led service
"Empathy drives our every move, ensuring we serve with heart and without judgment."

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of transparency and accountability. Every rupee donated, every program funded, and every outcome achieved is documented and shared openly with our donors and the communities we serve. We believe trust is earned through honesty — and we never take that for granted.

  • 100% financial transparency — annual reports published publicly
  • Zero-tolerance policy for corruption or misuse of funds
  • Independent audits conducted every year by certified bodies
  • Donors receive detailed impact reports on their contributions
"We are accountable to our donors, our communities, and above all, our conscience."

Commitment

Commitment means showing up — every single day, in every corner of the world where we are needed. We don't start programs and abandon them. We build lasting relationships with communities, ensuring our work creates sustainable change that outlives any single campaign or donation cycle.

  • 14+ years of continuous service since 2012
  • Long-term community partnerships — not one-time aid drops
  • Follow-up visits & outcome tracking for every program
  • 365-day operational presence in 15+ countries
"We don't just respond to crises — we commit to communities for the long haul."

Impact

We measure our success not by the size of our programs, but by the real difference they create in people's lives. Every initiative we launch is impact-driven — designed with measurable goals, tracked through data, and refined based on results. Good intentions only matter when they translate into real, lasting change.

  • 25,000+ lives directly improved across all programs
  • All programs have defined, measurable impact targets
  • Annual impact reviews shared publicly with full data
  • 150+ projects completed with verified outcomes
"Every dollar, every hour, every effort must create a ripple of real, measurable change."
